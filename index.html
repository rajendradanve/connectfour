Game Instruction

Connect Four game board has 6 rows (Horizontal) and 7 columns (vertical). This is 2 player game. So you can either play against computer or you can play with your friends. Left click on the column where player want to put the his coin. If you are playing against computer (as player 2), these moves will be played automatically.

Winning

Player who build first line with four his color coins is the winner. Line can be vertical, horizontal or diagonal.

So while you are trying to make your line you must think of blocking your opponents's move to avoid finishing his line.
