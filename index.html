Pokeball Connect Four

Against Whom You Are Playing?

Game Information

Pokeball Connect Four game board has 6 rows (Horizontal) and 7 columns (vertical). This is 2 player game. So you can either play on the computer or you can play with your family/friend. The first player has a red Pokeball coin and the second player has a yellow Pokeball coin. Left-click on the column where the player wants to put his coin. If you are playing against the computer (as player 2), these moves will be played automatically.

Winning

The player who builds the first line with four of his Pokeball coins is the winner. The line can be vertical, horizontal, or diagonal. See the below images. While you are trying to make your Pokeball line you need to make sure that your opponent doesn't finish his line first.
